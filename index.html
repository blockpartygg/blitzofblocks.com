A free-to-play competitive puzzle game. Slide blocks to make matches. Form combos and chains to amass the highest score.

Slide blocks left and right to form matches of 3 blocks.

Create combos by matching more than 3 blocks or chains by forming matches that lead to other matches.

Score as many points in 2 minutes as possible!
