A free-to-play competitive block-matching game. Slide blocks to form matches. Create combos and chains to amass the highest score.

Slide blocks left and right to form matches of 3 blocks, clearing way for more blocks to fall from above.

Create combos by matching more than 3 blocks or chains by forming matches that lead to other matches.

Earn points for every block matched, as well as bonuses for combos and chains. Score as many points in 2 minutes as possible!
